Turn Your Old Devices Into Opportunity

Donate your used phones, laptops, and tablets. We refurbish them and get them into the hands of students who need them most while keeping e-waste out of landfills.

Our Mission

Reducing e-waste. Bridging the gap.

We are a student-led initiative committed to sustainability and digital access. Every donated device gets a second life and helps students who otherwise could not afford the technology they need.

Keep devices out of landfills through responsible reuse and recycling.

How It Works

From Donation To Student Impact

A simple, transparent process that ensures devices are put to meaningful use.

1

Device Collected

Submit your old device details and photos. We review everything and confirm your donation.

2

Repair And Refurbish

If the device can be repaired, we restore it for reuse. If not, we responsibly recycle or repurpose parts.

3

Donated To Students

Refurbished devices are distributed to students who need reliable technology for learning.
