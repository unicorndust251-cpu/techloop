How It Works

From Donation To Student Impact

A simple, transparent process that ensures devices are put to meaningful use.

1

Device Collected

Submit your old device details and photos. We review everything and confirm your donation.

2

Repair And Refurbish

If the device can be repaired, we restore it for reuse. If not, we responsibly recycle or repurpose parts.

3

Donated To Students

Refurbished devices are distributed to students who need reliable technology for learning.
